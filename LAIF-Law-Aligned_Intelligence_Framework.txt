The Law-Aligned Intelligence Framework (LAIF) is a constitutional-level governance standard for AI systems.

It provides a unified test — coupling (alignment between restrictions and protected interests), consistency (applicability across scales), and reversibility (preservation of future decision capacity) — used to evaluate AI deployments, policies, and regulatory decisions.

LAIF is implemented operationally through the Pre-Deployment Coherence Assessment (PDCA), a structured audit process applied before system deployment.
